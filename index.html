Gin-Based

  • Made by Shake

    • More foamy and fluffy because of Egg White.

    1.3 Pink Lady

  • Dry Gin 40ml
  • Grenadine Syrup 20ml
  • Egg White 1
  • Made by Shake

    • Made to commemorate the stage “Pink Lady”, which originated in London and broke was a big hit. Given to the starring actress at the final party.

    1.4 Waikiki Beachcomber

  • Dry Gin 20ml
  • White Curaçao 20ml

  • Made by Shake

    • Pineapple taste makes it tropical. Orange flavor of White Curacao contributes refreshingly.

    1.5 Grenadine Fizz

  • Dry Gin 45ml
  • Lemon Juice 20ml
  • Grenadine Syrup 20ml
  • Sparkling Water
  • Maraschino Cherry & Lemon Slice (optional)
  • Made by Shake w/o soda, Build w/ soda, put cherry & lemon slice pierece by a pin

    • Refreshing taste made by mixing sweet grenadine syrup, lemon juice and soda.

    1.6 Gin Tonic

  • Dry Gin 45ml
  • Tonic Water
  • Lime Slice or Cut (optional)
  • Made by Build

    • Made with gin and tonic water poured over ice and garnished with a slice or cut of lime.

    1.7 Gimlet

  • Dry Gin 45ml
  • Lime Juice 15ml
  • Simple Syrup
  • Made by Shake

    • Turns to Vodka Gimlet by using Vodka instead of Dry Gin. Turns to Gimlet highball by adding Sparkling Water. Turns to Nobunaga by using Grenadine Syrup instead of Simple Syrup.

    1.8 Pink Pussycat

    • Gin and grapefruit juice go perfectly together in this sweet, pink-hued cocktail.

    Vodka-Based


    2.1 Moscow Mule

  • Lime Juice 15ml

  • Lime 3 cuts
  • Mint (optional)
  • Made by Build, decorate Lime properly

    • Typical Vodka-based cocktail. Originally Ginger Beer was used instead of Ginger Ale.

    2.2 Vodka Gimlet

  • Vodka 45ml
  • Lime Juice 15ml
  • Simple Syrup 1-3tsp
  • Made by Shake

    • Turns to Gimlet by using Dry Gin instead of Vodka.

    2.3 Kamikaze

  • Dry Gin 45ml
  • White Curaçao 20ml
  • Lime Juice 20ml
  • Made by Shake

    • Turns to Classic Kamikaze by changing fraction to 9:1:3 and using cocktail glass.

    2.4 Screwdriver

  • Vodka 45ml
  • Orange Juice 75ml
  • Maraschino Cherry (optional)
  • Made by Shake

    • The origin of the name is that it was originally build with a screwdriver. Turns to Creamy Driver by adding Sugar (1tsp) and Egg Yellow.

    2.5 Balalaika

  • Vodka 30ml
  • White Curaçao 15ml
  • Lemon Juice 15ml
  • Made by Shake

    • Balalaika is a Russian stringed instrument that is triangular like cocktail glass. Turns to Japanese White Lady by using Dry Gin instead of Vodka.

    3.4 Nadeshiko 撫子

  • Sake 45ml
  • Grenadine Syrup 2tsp
  • Lemon Juice 1tbsp
  • Simple Syrup 1tsp
  • Egg White 1/3
  • Made by Shake

    • Nadeshiko means pink flower, specifically “Dianthus superbus." It also means Yamato Nadeshiko (大和撫子), a Japanese term used to praise the unadorned, clean beauty, of a woman.
